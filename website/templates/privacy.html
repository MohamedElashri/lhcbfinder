Privacy Policy

Introduction

At LHCb Papers Search, we are committed to protecting your privacy while providing you with a valuable research tool. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

We collect and process minimal information necessary to maintain the security and performance of our service:

  • IP Address: We temporarily store IP addresses for security purposes and to prevent abuse of our services.
  • Access Timestamps: We record when our service is accessed to monitor and prevent potential misuse.
  • User Agent Information: We collect browser/client information to prevent automated scraping and ensure optimal service performance.

How We Use This Information

The collected information is used exclusively for:

  • Preventing abuse and maintaining service security
  • Protecting against automated scraping and bot activities
  • Ensuring continuous service availability
  • Improving service performance and reliability

Data Retention

We retain the collected data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy. Access logs are regularly rotated and old data is automatically purged.

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information beyond the technical data mentioned above. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any other user tracking technologies.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please contact us through the information provided on our About page.
